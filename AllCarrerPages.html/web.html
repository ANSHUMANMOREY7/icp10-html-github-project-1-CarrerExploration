What is Web Development?

Web development is like building your own digital universe, where you get to design the look, feel, and behavior of everything that happens on a website. Imagine crafting a storefront or an interactive app that users can engage with from anywhere in the world.


Job description & respnsibilities of web developer

A Web Developer is responsible for designing, coding, and maintaining websites and web applications. They use a mix of programming languages, frameworks, and tools to ensure that websites are functional, user-friendly, and visually appealing. Depending on the role, a web developer may focus on the front-end, back-end, or work across the full stack, handling both the client-facing and server-side aspects of a website.

Basic Requirement

    Developing websites and web applications

    Front-end development

    Back-end development

    Full-stack development
